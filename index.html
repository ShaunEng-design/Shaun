Welcome to our page


DesignX was established to address the need for affordable website solutions for small businesses.


Many small businesses, such as home bakeries or home maintenance companies, may not have the budget for high-end websites. These businesses often require simple yet effective websites that convey essential information without the need for costly features.

Competitive pricing


Some companies may charge between R3000 and R5000 for a basic 3-page website. At DesignX, we offer more affordable pricing options that are easier on your budget.

About Us


We specialize in designing and building streamlined static websites that prioritize simplicity and efficiency. Our goal is to create clear, concise pages that allow visitors to easily find the information they need without sifting through excessive text or images.


With straightforward navigation and well-organized content, our websites stand out by offering a user-friendly experience in contrast to the increasingly complex and flashy websites of today.

Our Mission


We are dedicated to helping small businesses establish an online presence. Even a simple website can make a big difference—by being accessible 24/7, it allows potential customers to easily see what you offer.


Our websites are straightforward and to the point. We focus on presenting the essential information clearly, so visitors can quickly understand who you are, what services or products you provide, and view any relevant examples, such as past maintenance projects, cakes, charms, or whatever your business specializes in. No frills, no fuss—just effective, clean design.
